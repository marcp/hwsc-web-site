Get Ready for Fall Soccer!


Online registration is now open for Hollywood Soccer Club!


We host recreational teams for boys and girls from Kindergarten through eighth grade. Our club welcomes participants new to soccer as well as experienced players who want to give our great program a try.


Registration is handled on a first-come basis. Sign up early to reserve a space for your player!


  • U7-U14: Register now and enjoy a $10 discount through June 30th! $75 per player ($85 starting July 1st).
  • Kinderkick: $55 per player. Kinderkick offers 1-hour midweek sessions, days and times TBD. It is only open to players currently enrolled in Kindergarten during the 2017-2018 school year and is not intended for younger Pre-K enthusiasts. See the FAQ for more details.
  • Financial Assistance: Our club offers scholarships. Please contact our registrars prior to registering your player if you seek financial assistance.
  • Scholarship Fund: If you'd like to support our club scholarship fund, please donate during registration checkout.


All families are encouraged to review our Team Formation Policy and seek answers to any soccer-related questions in the FAQ.


If you plan to coach in the Fall, either as an Assistant or Head Coach, please register now!


We welcome new coaches - contact our registrars if you'd like to sign up and have questions. It is your dedication that makes our club a great place to play.


Thank you for your continued support of Hollywood Soccer Club!


Kate Weaver and Donee Deschler

Questions?
